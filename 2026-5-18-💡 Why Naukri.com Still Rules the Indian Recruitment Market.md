💡 Why Naukri.com Still Rules the Indian Recruitment Market.

LinkedIn may be trendy, but when it comes to active hiring, Naukri.com remains the backbone.

Here’s why:
1️⃣ Identity vs. Intent
LinkedIn = professional branding, networking, passive opportunities
Naukri.com = structured workflows for active job seekers
2️⃣ Recruiter-Centric Model
Naukri.com is B2B at its core.
Recruiters develop habits, creating platform inertia that’s hard to replace.
3️⃣ Liquidity Over Design
In a marketplace, liquidity matters more than design.

Success comes from continuous interaction between recruiters and candidates.

Naukri.com proves that in recruitment, active talent + habit-building > modern interfaces. 
LinkedIn is great for networking, but when the job is real - Naukri.com runs the show.
