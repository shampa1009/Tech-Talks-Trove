💳 The Truth Behind “Lifetime Free” Credit Cards

Think our credit card is free? Think again.
Even with zero annual fees, banks make money through:
👉 Merchant commissions
👉 Interest & late fees
👉 EMI charges
👉 Cross-selling loans and insurance

📌 Paying just the minimum due may feel safe, but it can turn our card into a high-cost debt trap. Reward points aren’t freebies, they’re designed to keep us engaged.

💡 Nothing that looks free really is. Banks monetize our behavior and data, not the upfront fee.

Swipe wisely. Stay aware. Protect money.
