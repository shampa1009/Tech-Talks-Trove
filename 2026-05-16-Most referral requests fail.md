Most referral requests fail - not because people don’t want to help, but because we make them work.

When we ask someone to “let me know if you hear of any opportunities,” we’re actually giving them tasks:
 1️⃣ Scan their network
 2️⃣ Figure out how to describe us
And most people avoid extra work, even if they want to help.

Here’s the fix: shift the effort to ourselves.
Use the one-line method:
“If any opportunity comes to mind, then let me know please. I’ll draft a quick note and share the Resume/CV. You can simply just forward it.”
✅ Zero effort for them
✅ Faster responses

💡People don’t ignore us. They avoid extra work. Make it frictionless, and job referrals start flowing.
