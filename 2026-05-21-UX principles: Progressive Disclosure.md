One of the most underrated UX principles is: Progressive Disclosure.

Good products don’t show everything at once.
They show the right thing at the right moment.

Think about a food delivery app:
🍔 Choose restaurant
🍕 Choose food
📍 Enter address
💳 Payment
🎯 Simple. Step by step. No overwhelm.

Now imagine if the app opened with:
• Restaurant filters
• Delivery options
• Promo engine
• Wallet settings
• Invoice preferences
• Tax breakdown
• Restaurant filters
• Payment gateway configurations
…all on the first screen. Most users would leave instantly.

That’s why Progressive Disclosure matters.
It reduces cognitive load, improves focus, and helps users complete tasks with confidence.
