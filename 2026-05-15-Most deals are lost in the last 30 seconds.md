Most deals are lost in the last 30 seconds.

Why? Because we ask: “Do you have any questions?”
Sounds polite, but it slows momentum. Clients start thinking, not deciding.

Try this instead:
“Based on this, here’s what I recommend we do next.”
Walk them through the next step.

Deals don’t close on more info, they close when the next step is clear.
💡 Take control. Guide. Don’t question.
